Two Helpful Uncles Bopping to the Beat
Jenna Pitt looked at the ripped book in her hands and felt sad.
She walked over to the window and reflected on her beautiful surroundings. She had always loved grey Plymouth with its numerous, narrow nooks. It was a place that encouraged her tendency to feel sad.
Then she saw something in the distance, or rather someone. It was the figure of Alice Parker. Alice was an admirable deity with charming eyelashes and feathery elbows.
